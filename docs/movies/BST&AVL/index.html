BST and AVL

Welcome to the wild and wacky world of binary search trees (BSTs) and AVL trees! These are data structures that store data in a hierarchical way, allowing fast search, insertion, and deletion operations. But not all BSTs are created equal. Some of them can become unbalanced and lose their efficiency. Thats why we need AVL trees, which are BSTs that maintain a balance invariant by performing rotations when needed. Today, we will learn about operations on a BST, the AVL Invariant, the different types of rotations, and how to keep your AVLs balanced and stay alive. Lets get started!
